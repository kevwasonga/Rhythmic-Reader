Welcome to Rhythmic Reader!

This is a sample text to test the reading functionality.

The application will read this text line by line, highlighting each line as it speaks.

You can adjust the reading speed to match your preference.

The text-to-speech engine uses your browser's built-in voices.

This tool is designed to help improve reading comprehension and speed.

It's particularly useful for people with dyslexia or attention difficulties.

You can pause, resume, or skip lines using the control buttons.

Keyboard shortcuts are also available for easy navigation.

The application works offline once loaded, making it convenient to use anywhere.

Settings are saved locally in your browser for a personalized experience.

Try different themes and font options to find what works best for you.

Happy reading!
